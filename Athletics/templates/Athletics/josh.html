ABOUT JOSH J. LAL MEMORIAL

It is inter and intra college sports festival: Josh( intra college) and J.Lal(inter college) Memorial Tournament. Named after our eminent ex-Prime Minister, Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru,it was first held in 1996. A total of 14 events including basketball, badminton, volleyball, skating, carrom, chess, gym activities etc. are held . The event witnesses huge participation from the student community.
