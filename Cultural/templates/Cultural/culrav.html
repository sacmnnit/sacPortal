ABOUT CULRAV

The end of March will corroborate to be a week of euphoria and fresh ideation, a resplendent rostrum to hone ones flair and virtuosity and bask in the glory of triumph and merriment, known as CULRAV 2018- the annual cultural extravaganza of Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology Allahabad.

From the land of “unity in diversity” , festivities and tradition , the stage is set for an all new bigger and better spree, a compelling exhibition of creativity, innovation and celebrations! Setting the national trend for a youth festival with the stronger-than-ever CULturalRAVolution, Culrav 2018 promises to culminate and cultivate the whiz bang of talent from across the country! A fete for all, inspiring unity and setting a national platform to showcase a grand colorful mixture of talent, mettle and wit, this undeniably is the place to be!
